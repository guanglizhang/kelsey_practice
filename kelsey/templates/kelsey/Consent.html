Welcome to the consent form for this economics study. You must read the following carefully before checking the box next to the red informed consent statement below the text area to proceed with your participation.

Procedures:

You will be asked to make a series of decisions involving different payoffs. The entire session, which will include 18 separate decisions, split into two parts, will last up to 30 minutes. You will be given detailed information on how to make choices and your payment will depend on your decisions. The rules and payments may vary across parts and participants. For example, you may be asked to select a price at which to buy or sell an object, choose how to distribute a sum of money between participants, decide how much to donate to a public project, make predictions about other participants' decisions, or make a decision in some other context. This study follows the standards of experimental economics: participants must give their informed consent to participate, they are always paid, and they are never deceived.

Participation:

 You must be 18 years of age or older to participate. Your participation is voluntary, and you may choose not to participate or to withdraw from a study at any time and for any reason with no penalty.

Risks:

There are no known harms or discomforts beyond those encountered in the decision-making of normal daily life. There is no cost to you to participating.

Benefits:

 There are no direct benefits to you other than monetary payments, but benefits to society include the generalized knowledge gained about decision-making.

Privacy:

The only personal information that will be available to the researchers is what is publicly available on your MTurk profile and any information that you choose to provide during the course of the study. This information will not be shared with any individuals who are not part of the research team.

Confidentiality:

 Researchers will be given information about your decisions only. They will not be given any personal identifying information. Data on decisions will be stored indefinitely for data analysis. Your name will be kept separately from data on decisions and will be used for administrative purposes only, such as ensuring that you receive your payment. Any information derived from this research project that personally identifies you will not be voluntarily released or disclosed without your separate consent, except as specifically required by law.

Compensation:

Every participant is guaranteed at least $7 for participating in a session. Additional payments may also be received depending on the choices you make and factors determined randomly by the computer or by chance. Receipt of these additional payments is conditional upon completion of the experiment session. If you withdraw during the study or are withdrawn by the research team, then you will still receive the show-up payment. Your payment is made privately using the details you have provided to Mechanical Turk. The only form of compensation is the monetary payment. You will not receive an MTurk rating for this experiment.

Contact:

If you have questions or concerns, please contact the researchers at tufts.usc.study@gmail.com or or contact the Human Subjects Committee at UCSB at hsc@ucsb.edu or 805-893-3807. The study procedures are reviewed by the UCSB Human Subjects Committee to ensure compliance with established standards of human subject protection.

Consent:

 By checking the box below next to the red informed consent statement, you acknowledge that you have read the rules and privacy policy, you certify you are 18 years of age or older, and you agree that your participation is voluntary.
